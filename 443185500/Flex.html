Cala Gonone

Cala Gonone is located in Sardinia, Italy and is a place of stunning beauty. With a rugged coastline stretching for miles and clear turquoise water, this is one of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

Champagne Beach

Located in Vanuatu, Champagne beach is known for its crystal waters and powdery white sand. With it's beautiful scenery this is a must for any visitors to Vanuatu. The waves reflect the name and have beautiful frothy spume.

Wai'napanapa Black Sand Beach

This is a gorgeous volcanic beach with black sand located on the island of Maui, Hawaii. Backed by lava cliffs and lush green vegetation, this is one of the best black sand beaches in the world.

Whitehaven Beach

Located near the Whitsunday islands, this beach is known for its startlingly white white sand and its proximity to the Great Barrier Reef. This beach also has a lovely lighthouse walk from which there is an amazing view.

Praia Da Pipa Beach

Praia Da Pipa Beach is one of the most famous beaches in Brazil, close to the town of Pipa. The literal translation of the beach is "Kite Beach". It has beautiful cliffs and is the surfers paradise due to strong waves.
